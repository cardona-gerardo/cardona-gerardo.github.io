Research interests

I am currently working on control algorithms for quantum systems, and investigating the role of active quantum feedback for the protection of quantum information. More generally I'm interested in problems related to

  • Open quantum systems and quantum trajectories
  • Quantum information and quantum error correction
  • Stability of Markov processes
  • Stochastic control problems (Martingale methods, dynamic programming,...)
